How to Buy

  • Download Metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google Play store for free. Desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by going to metamask.io.
  • Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $POF. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on Metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.
  • Switch ETH for $POF. You may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
